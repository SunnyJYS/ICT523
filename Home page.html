About Us

We are the largest higher education music academy in Australia with a history of 50 years in the field of music education. As a world-class independent education institution for music, entertainment, and performing arts, AcadeWe enjoys a high reputation in the industry. We will use all profits to improve students' learning experience and ensure that our courses keep up with the times, in order to respond to the constantly changing demands of the industry.

Courses

Musical theater

Musical performance mainly studies the theory of modern stage art and the basic skills of singing, dancing and performance. It has strong ability to shape characters, create, arrange and direct. It can independently and creatively complete the character performance of musical plays and stage plays, as well as the performance and creation of short play musical skits.

Music Theory

Key and difficult points in learning and understanding the generation, nature, and musical sound system of sounds: grouping of sounds, equipotency, natural sound level, and variable sound level.

Music Ensemble

In the ensemble class, a piece of music is jointly performed by several students, each responsible for their own voice parts. Unlike a solo piece, in an ensemble, if there is a lack of students playing any part, this ensemble piece is incomplete. In the performance of a ensemble, a student may sometimes play the main melody and sometimes as an accompaniment. Each ensemble student is an indispensable and important member in the performance of this ensemble.
